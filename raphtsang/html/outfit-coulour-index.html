Colour in Outfits

July 2022 - November 2022

Colour Studio - GDES20002

Skills

Graphic Design, Colour Composition

Tools

HTML, CSS, Javascript, Adobe Illustrator

Overview

Creating an index of the outfits I wore over the span of 12 weeks, I took a picture of my outfit 3 days a week, where each of the 3 days were different occasions. These are: work, class, and the weekend.

Each of these outfits were then reduced down to their colours, creating outfit palettes that omit most factors of the original outfits' form. Retaining only simplistic blocks of colour, my journey through colour can be clearly seen and analysed without distraction.

Two outfits and their colours
Top left: Work Outfit, Week 8, Bottom left: Class Outfit, Week 8, Top right: Weekend Outfit, Week 9, Bottom right: Class Outfit, Week 5

Aside from mapping the journey of my outfits through static compositions, a website was also created summarizing colour in my outfits. The site allows the user to search for basic and specific colours (try khaki or beige), weeks, and occasions (work/class/weekend), and will return all of the outfit colour palettes that fit the description searched. Not only can the website give all visitors an overview of my journey through colour in my outfits, I also use it myself to search for outfit ideas based off of colour or activity, and for general reference in my daily life, as hovering over each palette displays information of the original week and activity it was worn.
