Neon + Cyberpunk

July 2022 - November 2022

Colour Studio - GDES20002

Skills

3D Modelling, Voxel Modelling

Tools

MagicaVoxel, Adobe Photoshop

Overview

Neon lights and bright flashing signs have always been a fascination for me. Growing up in Hong Kong, I used to be mesmerized at night, looking upward not at the moon or stars, but at the rows of glowing signs that jut out over the street, outshining the dim night sky.

In this project, I sought to explore the use of lights and neon in colour and was inspired not only by the lights of my hometown, but also by the aesthetic and graphics of the game Cyberpunk 2077.

Concept

Cyberpunk 2077 is an open-world game set in a futuristic dystopian world, where society has taken on high tech in a poor world, resulting in a unique aesthetic that utilizes many holograms and bright neon lights without appearing overly refined or futuristic. I was captivated by the concept and look of the game even before its release. Promotional videos and marketing of the game featured a heavy focus on the appearance of the game, detailing the reflections of neon signs in puddles on the in-game streets, and the glow of the lights through the haze of the sleazy streets.

Design Process

Wanting to explore how these aspects of atmosphere and environment would affect the lights and the colours they bring, I set out to create my own world based on the premise of the game, and ultimately landed on a voxel art depiction of the main character, V's, apartment.

I chose to use a voxel medium to realise the composition as I felt that it was able to capture the general look of the game's features, while at the same time adding to the depiction of colours and light in the work, as the simplistic, pixel/voxel forms helped to keep audiences focused on the colours of the piece and the interaction of light. Lessening the distraction of the other elements and forms in the room puts emphasis on the aspect of colour in neon lights, where Cyberpunk 2077 was purely used as a medium for its interpretation.

While some of the neon signs in the work were found in the room, some others were taken from the wider game world to create a more vibrant and engaging final composition, allowing for more interesting interactions between signs that aren't usually found in the same vicinity.

Final Details + Renders

The resulting animation features lights that reflect off of mirrors and metallic surfaces and lights that get diffused on matte surfaces. The foggy atmosphere amplifies the flickering of lights as the colours are scattered and fade away, especially near the karaoke sign. The inclusion of the holographic unit near the foreground of the room plays with transparency and portrays light in an almost tangible form that is not usually seen. It is also interesting to see how contrasting colours interact with each other, as the colour shifts from purple to green below the desk, and the blue and pink lights in the corner come together.
