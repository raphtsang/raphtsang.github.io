Overview

A concept for a vinyl release of slchld's single, say what's on your mind.

The record labels, cover, sleeve, as well as other collateral such as VHS stylized stills were all considered in this reimagined release of his single, and even merchandise such as hoodies and tees were mocked-up to convey the full potential of the designs.

Inspiration

I was initially inspired by Peter Saville and his appropriation of factory posters and inspiration from Jan Tschichold's Die Neue Typography when designing The Factory posters and for Factory Records. I took this and applied it to my surroundings, and combined it with the current craze for vintage, such as clothing and technologies such as film cameras and vinyl records. Through an iterative and explorative process, I landed upon a particular design of Polaroid's Super Colour Video Casette packaging, and experimented with basing the design of slchld's single around this concept.
